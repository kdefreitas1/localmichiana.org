About

Welcome to Local Michiana!

This website is a source to promote different things happening in the Michiana community.

General Information

Local Michiana is meant to connect people with the community. We promote all kinds of events, things to do, and places to go. We believe in the importance of connecting with the community, and want to provide ease of access for that.

What We Offer

  • Event Listings: Stay informed about upcoming events, festivals, and activities in the area.
  • Places to Go: Learn more about all kinds of different places to go
  • Community Focus: We prioritize helping promote the local Michiana community.

Our Story

Local Michiana was created in one semester by Kamalani DeFreitas with the help of Sohan Patel. The goal was to create an open source website that could help promote the local community. More information can be found on this github page.
